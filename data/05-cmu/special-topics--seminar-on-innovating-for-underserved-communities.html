Description:

The ultimate goal of this course is to familiarize students with ongoing work in the area of technology innovations for underserved communities. This seminar course will review several key publications and presentations on this topic and teach students the necessary skills to analyze and critique this work in useful ways. Class sessions will be dedicated to discussing reading/viewing assignments and each class discussion will be led by students. Assignments will involve analyzing, writing, and presenting critiques of specific projects.
